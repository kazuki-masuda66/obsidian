### 11. BL Info（B/L情報とSea Waybill）

#### Sea Waybill
- **Sea waybill**: Sea waybill is globally accepted as one of the shipping document substituting bill of lading. However, there are some countries which do not accept usage of sea waybill for some reason.
- **Bangladesh**: Bangladesh is one of them.

#### Bangladesh
- **SWB**: In Bangladesh, sea waybill (SWB) is a non-negotiable document and it cannot be accepted for L/C negotiation by the Authorized Dealer (AD) Bank for realization of export proceed. As such SWB is not recognized as per Bangladesh Foreign Exchange Control Regulation.
- **Bangladesh Foreign Exchange Regulation Act 1947**: The main purpose is to ensure that the Bills of Lading are only sent to the Buyers and that Cargo is only delivered to the Buyers after the Sellers have received payment of the full Cargo value through the AD. It stipulates that in respect of export of goods from Bangladesh to foreign countries, the Bills of Lading should be drawn only to the order of an AD designated for this purpose by the respective exporters and delivered to the authorized representatives of the AD concerned and to none else.

#### ONE's Procedure for Bangladesh
- **ONE will NOT accept**: With reference to local regulation, to protect ONE's interest, ONE will NOT accept the issuance of Sea Waybill from Bangladesh without following guidance:
- **Approval Process**:
  - a. All request from the customer must be endorsed by the ONE (BD) country head
  - b. Depending on the customer classifications (GKA (Global Key Account) or non-GKA)) there are additional requirements for ONE agent to provide financial reports and LOI
  - c. All agreement should be reviewed on annual basis by the ONE agent and provide feedback to RHQ MKT
  - d. The authorizing parties are GSM and SYM for GKA accounts and champion trade for non-GKA accounts
- **Note**: In addition to sea waybill, generally, straight BL is also prohibited for the shipment from Bangladesh. For further details, please check with Bangladesh office before proceed to action.

#### Prohibited Countries
- **Prohibited Countries**: Please note that ONE will not accept issuing sea waybill from/to below listed Country since sea waybill is not accepted under each local laws and regulations.:
  - Nicaragua
  - Honduras
  - Guatemala
  - Brazil
  - Iran (Under Sanction Guideline, currently ban any shipment)
  - Colombia

#### ONE's Procedure（一般）
- **Legally**: As mentioned before, legally, except the prohibited countries and Bangladesh, there is no issue to issue sea waybill in general. However, for customer's credibility and trustworthy control, SYM set each RHQ to have an authority to approve sea waybill issuance after considering customer's credibility and trustworthy.
- **RHQ authority**: RHQ may give blanket approval to each country management which means each country has the authority to approve based on the market and customers.

#### Place of Delivery vs Final Destination
- **Correct Answer**: 3. Place of Delivery is for ONE to deliver the cargo and Final Destination is no meaning to ONE since it is already beyond ONE's responsibility.
- **Explanation**: Carrier's responsibility is from Port of Receipt up to Place of Delivery.
- **ONE's B/L Terms and Conditions**:
  - "Place of Delivery" means a place so named overleaf or any other place where the Carrier has contracted to deliver the Goods when such place is other than the Port of Discharge.
  - "Final Destination" means any particular are insert solely at the request of the Merchant for his convenience and reference only. Carrier shall not be responsible to deliver the cargo to this destination.

#### Final Destination on ONE BL
- **In view of above**: In view of above, we shall not insert details of Final Destination on ONE's BL as this is not related to the contract of carriage between ONE and cargo interest. However, we note that customer might request some special wording: in transit to XX or final destination YY to comply Letter of Credit's requirement.
- **From legal point of view**: From legal point of view, below suggestion to fulfill the request of customer.
- **1. "Export Reference" and "Final Destination" boxes**: These 2 boxes are designed to state special customer's requirement. Any particulars inserted in these 2 boxes are solely for customer's convenience and reference.
