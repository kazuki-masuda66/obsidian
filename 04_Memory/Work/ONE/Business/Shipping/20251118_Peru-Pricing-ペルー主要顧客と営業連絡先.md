### Nature of PERU customers
1. **almost 99% are on FAK (Short term)**: ペルーの顧客のほとんどはFAK（短期契約）
2. **Hiraoka is on bi monthly**: Hiraokaは隔月契約。Quoting pattern usually is T1 or USD50/TEU lower than T1 as max。Need to guide on the HIRAOKA file and quotation pattern
3. **TOYOTA PERU (mainly ex TH) is the only long term deal (yearly)**: TOYOTA PERU（主にタイ発）が唯一の長期契約（年間契約）
4. **Occasional receive request into PAITA**: 時々PAITAへのリクエストを受ける。Paita generally needs 40RH。Add on is USD600 on top of PECLL rates
5. **PERU import majority is 40', but PERU export is mainly 20'**: ペルー輸入は主に40フィート、ペルー輸出は主に20フィート
