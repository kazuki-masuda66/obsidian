# DYM-Change-Management-team-VisionとTeam-member-Role

## 概要
DYM Change Management teamに関するメモ。Team vision、Team value、Team member's role and responsibility、Target Scope、Task Management、Design / Tool policyについて記録されています。

## 内容

### Team vision
- **Vision:** Create a state where every single M&C user has a **deep understanding of DYM/SYM's projects** (Digital solution and processes etc.) and is **proactive about making changes** in their own work with project members.
- **Increase awareness**
- **Provide high quality contents**
- **Motivate users**

### Team value
1. **User satisfaction**
   - Always think about what user need from the user's perspective.
   - Let's exceed user's expectations.
2. **Teamwork**
   - Let's work together as a team to provide a better contents
   - Collaborate with each project member, Com team, etc.
3. **Innovation**
   - Let's use our own cutting-edge technology to stay ahead of our users.
   - Let's be creative
