# SCF-RFA-TAAルール-Standard-SC-Format-10-Alphanumeric-Characters

## 概要
SCF/RFA/TAAルールに関するメモ。Standard SC Format、10 Alphanumeric Characters、Single Year、Multi Year、Office Prefix、Sequence、NVO or BCO、Year SC Created、NA signed GKA SCs、GCM、Previous Logic、SCのロジック by NAについて記録されています。

## 内容

### Standard SC Format: 10 Alphanumeric Characters

### 1. Single Year (validty = less than 12 months)
- **<Office Prefix> (3 Characters) + <Sequence> (4 digits) + <N>VO or <B>CO + <Year SC Created> (2 digits)**
- **Example: NYC0360N21**

### 2. Multi Year (validty = more than 12 months)
- **<Office Prefix> (3 Characters) + <Sequence> (4 digits) + <N>VO or <B>CO + <Year SC Created> (2 digits)**
- **Example: NYC0360N21**

### Note: For NA signed GKA SCs, the <Office Prefix> will be GCM
