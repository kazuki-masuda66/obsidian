## DX week in Latin RHQ - Eagle X

GHQ DYM conducted a rollout session with Latin RHQ members on the various digital projects which DYM is implementing now. (May 18-26)

In particular, on May 18-19, Eagle X project, which covers the end-to-end process of long-term contracts, has been rolled out with LA pricers and sales representatives from the Latin region.

During these two days, more than 40 sales and pricers learned how to use the Eagle X tools and understand the processes through actual hands-on training.  Various questions were solved and overall project directions were aligned!

Thank you to all LA Pricer and sales for the successful meeting. Special thanks to LA SYM members for hosting this DX week together with us.

We will continue to work with sales and pricers under the Eagle X project to make their day-to-day operations more and more efficient through digitization and standardization of processes.
