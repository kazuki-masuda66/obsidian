**Vision:** Create a state where every single M&C user has a **deep understanding of DYM/SYM's projects** (Digital solution and processes etc.) and is **proactive about making changes** in their own work with project members.

- Increase awareness
- Provide high quality contents
- Motivate users

- **Innovation**
